What can you gain MENTALLY from sports and exercise

Stress Relief

Exercise and other physical activity produce endorphins which are chemicals in your body that reduce your perception of pain and trigger a positive feeling in the body

Anxiety and Depression

As two of the primary mental issues plaguing our society many look to see what they can do in order to reduce the effects of the two disorders, look no further than the ease of just playing a sport or exercising. Rather it be the adrenaline rush of playing the sport or the large release of endorphins during and after exercising.

Sharper memory and thinking

The same endorphins that make you feel better also help you concentrate and feel mentally sharp for tasks at hand. Exercise also stimulates the growth of new brain cells.

Better self perception and self-esteem

As you play sports or exercise you will start to feel more confident about your appearance and, by meeting goals that you set, will feel more confident in your abilities

Resilience to hardship

Both sports and exercise have their hardships when it comes to pushing yourself, doing this over and over will start to build a resiliency to hardship that you can carry into almost everything else in your life
