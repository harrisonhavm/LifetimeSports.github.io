Recommended Sports

Finding out what sport to play can be very difficult, especially if you are not the one playing the sport so we’ve curated a list with pros and cons to help you make this decision

Basketball

Pros

  • Very Popular
  • Common sport to play outside of club/recreational
  • Good exercise
  • Fun game
  • Light learning curve

Cons

  • Injuries are very common
  • Very competitive
  • Talent is a huge factor

Swimming

Pros

  • Very good for your health
  • Builds on a life skill
  • Able to play in all seasons
  • For all ages

Cons

  • Large exposure to chlorine
  • Very thin margins for improvement
  • Harsh skill curve

Golf

Pros

  • Less physically demanding
  • A game that could be played in late age
  • Very Relaxing
  • Good money for Pro scene

Cons

  • Does little for exercise
  • Tough to learn and grow
  • Season dependent
  • Expensive

Football

Pros

  • Very Popular
  • Builds very good physique
  • Very involved with teammates
  • Common sport to play outside of club/recreational

Cons

  • Very likely to get injuries
  • Hard to play professionally
  • Extremely competitive

Baseball

Pros

  • Very Popular
  • Popular and Wealthy pro scene
  • Not intensely physically demanding

Cons

  • Time-consuming
  • Harsh Learning curve
  • Very Competitve

Soccer

Pros

  • Most popular sport in the world
  • Little gear required
  • Builds good physique

Cons

  • Likely to get injury
  • Very competitive
  • Unlikely to reach pro scene

Hockey

Pros

  • Great Exercise
  • Learn great balance and control
  • Easier Pro scene to get into

Cons

  • Injuries are very common
  • Extremely physically demanding
  • Lots of equipment needed

Tennis

Pros

  • Stamina building
  • Able to play in old age
  • Large Age range

Cons

  • Smaller Pro scene
  • Does little for exercise
  • Expensive

Gymnastics

Pros

  • Builds great physique
  • Increases coordination, flexibility and strength
  • Cool physical talents

Cons

  • Large injury Risk
  • Virtually no pro scene
  • Diet and Eating disorders
