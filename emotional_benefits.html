What can you gain EMOTIONALLY from sports and exercise

Teamwork

Most Sports require a lot of teamwork and coordination and over time you will build up important life skills such as communication, organization, problem solving, etc.

Playing by the rules

Playing by the rules is always very harshly taught to anyone who plays sports and cheaters always end up being caught. Playing sports teaches people the importance of rules and laws and the punishments that follow breaking them.

Self-Confidence

Similar to the mental benefit of raising self-esteem, playing sports raises Self-Confidence not only by the release of endorphins but also by achieving small goals as you play.

Controlling Emotions

As any good coach will tell you, always try to keep your emotions under control. Playing sports especially when surrounded by good coaches will teach you how to keep your emotions under control

Teammates

It doesn't matter if you play Recreationally, for your school, or even in a club, as long as you play a sport you will be playing with or against kids roughly your age and typically around where you live. It is also not unusual for you to form friendships with these same people seeing as you already have a similar interest as them
