About Me

Hello My name is Harrison H., I am currently 18 and have played sports for 15 of those years, from kicking a soccer ball in Miami to being a coach for a swim team. I have loved playing and being involved in all sorts of sports my whole life. There is probably not a sport that I haven't played after I have heard of it which is due to my competitive nature and my true love for all sports. Rather it be playing or just watching, sports has become part of my daily life.

About the Website

As stated in the Website Goal I hope that this website can get more people and specifically more children involved in sports. I have seen such a difference sports can make in people and seriously think that everyone should at least try one sport at least once or even just play a sport with some friends it does not matter which one. Everyone always pushes others to exercise but running or lifting weights can be boring sometimes so what better way to exercise than to have fun doing it playing a sport..
